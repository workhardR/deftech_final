About DEFTECH

India's Defence Knowledge Platform

DEFTECH is India's premier online educational portal dedicated to defence education and technology. We bridge the gap between military knowledge and civilian understanding — offering structured, accurate, and engaging content for students, researchers, and professionals.

Our platform covers DRDO research, missile systems, radar technology, military rank structures, and the future of Indian defence innovation — all curated by defence enthusiasts and subject-matter experts.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratize knowledge about India's defence ecosystem and empower the next generation of defence professionals, researchers, and informed citizens.

🛡️ Our Vision

A nation where every citizen understands and appreciates the technological prowess and strategic depth of India's armed forces and defence research.

📖 Our Content

Rigorously researched, publicly available information structured into digestible learning modules with interactive assessments and progress tracking.

🔬 DRDO Focus

Special emphasis on DRDO's landmark programmes — Agni, Prithvi, BrahMos, AESA radar, ATAGS howitzer, and emerging defence technologies.

India's Defence Journey

Key Milestones

🚀
1958
DRDO Established

Defence Research and Development Organisation founded to drive self-reliance in defence technology.

☢️
1998
Pokhran-II Nuclear Tests

Operation Shakti — India demonstrated thermonuclear and fission device capability, ushering in a new strategic era.

🎯
2001
BrahMos Missile

Joint India-Russia supersonic cruise missile — the world's fastest operational cruise missile at Mach 2.8.

⭐
2019
Mission Shakti — ASAT Test

India became the 4th country to demonstrate anti-satellite capability, shooting down a live satellite in LEO.

✈️
2024+
AMCA & Future Systems

Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft, FINSAS, directed energy weapons, and hypersonic glide vehicles under development.
